Living without stuff

A blog that you absolutely do not need

We are constantly beoing told what we should do and the things we need be experts in every field. Our whole lives are saturated with mantras such as

  • "Don't leave your job without another lined up"
  • "You should brush your teeth with fluoride toothpaste twice a day"
  • "Buying a house is a great investment"
  • "Watch your cholesterol"
  • "Good things come to those who wait"

It's tiring, overwhelming, and a great deal of it is misleading.

This blog breaks the monotony of accepting everything you hear by showing you how some of the things that we take for granted as truth are wrong, and how living without them can leave us better off in life.
