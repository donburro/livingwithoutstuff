Goal: increase DoGL

Everybody wants to live a better life.

It doesn't matter who you are, how you started, or what you think. The one thing that everybody wants to do is to have a better life.

If your life sucks right now, you're probably working to make it better. (^1: Unless you're lazy. Then you're just hoping it will magically get better without any effort on your parts.)

If your life is the best it will ever be right now, you're working to keep it that way.

Think about what your parents want for you. What do they want?

They want you to have a better life than they had. That's why they went through all of that trouble and hardship. It was for you. You will carry on their legacy. (^2: Unfortunately, today there are many parents who don't care for their children as they naturally should. A topic for another time, but I'd like to believe it is still the exception rather than the norm.)

Think about what you want for your children.

Barring a tragedy, they are going to live on this Earth much longer than you are. You'd like to leave the world a better place for them so that they can have an even better life than you have today.

Do you agree?

Do this thought experiment with me

Pretend that there are ten people on Earth, each with a certain Degree of Good Life (DoGL). DoGL measures how good a person's life is. It is the combination of health, relationships, money, freedom - all things that determine how good a life is. Each person's DoGL can go up or down. In the beginning, everybody has the same DoGL.

We'll represent the world's average DoGL level with a drawn line.

One very effective way of improving your life, as we know, is to improve the lives of other people, because it is a human trait to help people that help you.

The saying, "treat others the way you'd like to be treated" is an age old saying with a lot of wisdom in it.

If you recall from the earlier post (NEEDS TO BE WRITTEN AND LINKED, specialization is really what enables a society to grow and flourish.

Year 1

In our society of ten, we find that Penine (Person 9) enjoys a specialization in food acquistion. Penine loves to hunt and gather, but ends up wasting a lot of food because Penine has terrible food preparation skills. Penine is not very keen on improving because food is plentiful and it's not something he enjoys.

Peight, taking notice of this, proposes a partnership. Peight likes to prepare food, but only hunts out of necessity. Penine will gather food enough for both of them, and Peight will prepare meals more delicious than Penine has ever known. Peight and Penine agree to work together.

Peight is glad to not have to hunt, an Penine is relieved to not have to cook.

Year 2

Peight has always wanted to learn how to craft, but has never found the time because he was just so bad at hunting.

One day, Peight encounters Pseven (silent P) collecting berries into a masterfully crafted basket and asks if she can teach him how to craft. In exchange, Peight will provide Pseven food. A basket will also help Penine bring home more food. Pseven agrees.

While out on a hunt, Penine overhears someone practicing his comedy routine. He starts laughing because of how funny it is, and invites the young man, Russel Petens, to dine with them.

Since Pseven, Peight, and Penine have been working together, there is an abundance of food, and each is willing to provide for Russel as long as he continues to entertain them. This is very good for Russel, because he sucks at hunting, cooking, crafting - it is a real miracle that he has managed to survive as long as he has.

Somewhere else, Pethree taught Pefour how to tie his shoelaces so he wouldn't constantly trip. Pefour was so thankful that he gave Pethree his favorite book. Pethree learned a little something from it.

DoGLs have increased!

Years 3 through 8

Pesix has a knack for taming wild animals, and Petwo excels at construction, which helps food production because livestock is easier to catch.

Pefive is very creative and produces blueprints for Petwo to build better infrastructure for everybody.

Etc, etc, etc.

As Peone through Russel continue to find new ways to help each other, their DoGLs continue to rise.

After eight years, everyone's DoGL has reached exactly the same level. But this level is over twice as high as it was just eight years ago.

Amazing!

I'd call this an increase in humanity's potential. Everybody has worked with each other - both directly and indirectly - to improve the collective DoGL.

In fact, I believe progressing this way is the best way to progress, because everybody is satisfied with the amount of DoGL that they and - maybe even more important - everyone else has. No one has much more than they do, so there's no need to be jealous No one has much less, so no need to be protective.

As time goes by

Peight and Penine start to feel that they deserve more.

After all, they do supply the food. Everybody needs food. If they were to stop supplying food, everybody's DoGL would decrease dramatically as they would have to go back to supplying their own food.

In a way, it was their partnership that enabled the rapid increase in DoGL enjoyed by all.

They started it.

Why shouldn't they have a better life?

After all, they do supply the food...

Peight and Penine make a new agreement. The agree that because they control the means of (food) production, they should have a higher DoGL than all those benefitting from their work.

Except Russel. He's such a funny guy, and they don't want to lose the entertainment.

They tell Pseven, who has developed the infrustructure for food delivery to the masses, that times are tough and that more resources are needed in order to keep up with feeding everybody.

Pseven delivers the news to the people on his next food delivery. They are afraid that food may run low, and agree to help in any way that they can. Besides, they have no reason to doubt Peight and Penine. They get together and devise a plan.

Peone agrees to take on the duties of his friends - in addition to his own - as they work overtime to keep the food supply stable.

Pefour begins reading up on how to be more efficient in farming, hunting, taming animals, and construction. He serves as an advisor to Pefive, Petwo, and Pesix.

Pefive draws up plans for a magnificent facility that is capable of producing food up the ying yang.

Petwo gets it built on time and under budget.

Pesix helps to domesticate several new species of animal. He even learned from Pefour that crops can be crossbred to have more desirable traits, such as size.

Pethree, now a master of operations, increases the efficiency of all aspects of food production so that the task now requires less time, but provides much, much more.

After several years of toil, the new food production system is up and running. Peight and Penine thank everyone for their sacrifice, and that because of their hard work, there will likely be no food shortages in the coming years.

Peight and Penine, their jobs now almost completely automated, sit back and enjoy they fruits of their comrades' labor. And Russel's jokes, of course.

How great a DoGL they now have.

Pseven, who frequents the facility to pick up food, overhears Russel making a joke about the small mindedness of the masses. Penine and Peight erupt in laughter. Intrigued, he creeps closer to get a better listen.

As the laughter subsides, Pseven overhears:

Can you believe they fell for it? They gave up so much of their lives because they thought the food was running short. We didn't even have to feig a shortage. They willingly helped us reach this elite status! Hahahaha

Flabbergasted, Pseven storms in and confronts the triad. He threatens to tell everybody what they did.

The triad can't have that happen. If the masses found out about the deception, they would revolt.

They make another deal. The triad agrees to elevate Pseven's DoGL as long as he can keep a secret.

The truth always comes to light

If you have ever told a lie, you know that it never ends well for you. It might take a day, a month, a year, or ever several generations, but the truth will come out.

That is because people have an innate ability to detect when something is amiss. To know this, just look within. You certainly have come across something recently that just didn't feel right in your gut.

Persons 1-6, tired of being left behind while only a "select few" are able to live life to its fullest, take to the streets in protest.

As unrest grows, the elite begin to realize that they were too greedy. They tried to take too much, too fast, without giving back to the rest.

And now they've turned against them.

And they're outnumbered.

They start to beg for forgiveness, for mercy, but the masses have had enough.

The masses are out for blood.

Person 4 goes in first. He gets a massive blow on the side of the head that sends the last bit of pain he'll ever feel down his spine.

Others follow and finish the raid.

And what's left is not a pretty picture. Years of progress wiped away in a single fit of collective rage.

The DoGL average drops for the first time in history. Those remaining have to pick up the peices and continue on.

DoGL is greatly decreased. But at least the greedy bastards are gone so we won't be suppressed any more.

There are many different ways DoGL can be measured. You could say that the more wealthy you are, the more DoGL you have. You could equate the number of options you have, or the amount of free time you have in your life to DoGL.

A higher DoGL is what we are all looking for. We all want to increase our DoGL.

The ones on the right are already working on raising the avg.
