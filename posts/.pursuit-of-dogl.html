Goal: increase DoGL

Everybody wants to live a better life.

It doesn't matter who you are, how you started, or what you think. The one thing that everybody wants is to have a better life.

If your life sucks right now, you're working to make it better. (^1: Unless you're lazy. Then you're just hoping it will magically get better without any effort on your part.)

If your life is the best it will ever be right now, you're working to keep it that way.

Think about what your parents want for you. What do they want?

They want you to have a better life than they had. That's why they went through all of that trouble and hardship in their lives. It was for you. They want you to carry on and grow their legacy. (^2: Unfortunately, today there are many parents who don't care for their children as they naturally should. A topic for another time, but I believe it is still the exception.)

Think about what you want for your children.

Barring a tragedy, they are going to live on this Earth much longer than you are. You'd like to leave the world a better place for them so that they can have an even better life than you have today.

Do you agree?

Do this thought experiment with me

Pretend that there are ten people on Earth, each with a certain Degree of Good Life (DoGL). DoGL is a measure of how good a person's life is. It is the combination of health, relationships, money, freedom - all of the things that determine how good a life is. Each person's DoGL can go up or down.

In the beginning, everybody has the same DoGL:

We'll represent the world's average DoGL level with a drawn line.

One very effective way of improving your life, as we know, is to improve the lives of other people, because it is a human trait to return favors.

The saying, "treat others the way you'd like to be treated" is an age old saying with a lot of wisdom in it.

If you recall from the earlier post (NEEDS TO BE WRITTEN AND LINKED, specialization is really what enables a society to grow and flourish.

Using your specialization to benefit others will be the cornerstone for this thought experiment.

Year 1

In our society of ten, we find that Penine (Person 9) enjoys a specialization in food acquistion. Penine loves to hunt and gather, but ends up wasting a lot of food because Penine has terrible food preparation skills. Penine is not very keen on improving this though, because food is plentiful and Penine does not like preparing food.

Peight, taking notice of this, proposes a partnership. Peight likes to prepare food, hunting only out of necessity. He suggests that Penine gather food enough for both of them, and he will prepare meals more delicious than Penine has ever known. Penine and Peight agree to work together.

Peight is glad to not have to hunt, an Penine is relieved to not have to cook. The partnership results in an increased DoGL for both of them.

Year 2

Peight has always wanted to learn how to craft, but has never found the time because he was just so bad at hunting that finding food took up all of his time.

One day, Peight encounters Pseven (the P is silent) collecting berries into a masterfully crafted basket and asks if she can teach him how to craft. In exchange, Peight will provide Pseven food. Pseven agrees.

The baskets and things that Peight ends up making help Penine bring home more food, so there is enough for all three many times over.

While out on a hunt one day, Penine overhears someone practicing his comedy routine, and starts laughing because of how funny it is. He invites the young man, Russel Petens, to dine with them.

Since Pseven, Peight, and Penine have been working together, there is an abundance of food, and each is willing to provide for Russel as long as he continues to entertain them. This is very good for Russel, because he sucks at hunting, cooking, and crafting. It is a miracle that he has managed to survive as long as he has.

Somewhere else, Pethree taught Pefour how to tie his shoelaces so he wouldn't constantly trip. Pefour was so thankful that he gave Pethree his favorite book. Pethree learned a little something from it.

DoGLs have increased!

We draw a new average DoGL line:

Years 3 through 8

Pesix has a knack for taming wild animals, and Petwo excels at construction, which helps food production because livestock is easier to catch when you build a fence around them.

Pefive is very creative and produces blueprints for Petwo to build better infrastructure for food production, living, and recreation.

No need to bore you with the minor details. You get the picture.

As Peone through Russel continue to find new ways to help each other, their DoGLs continue to rise.

After eight years, everyone's DoGL has reached exactly the same level. But this level is over twice as high as it was just eight years ago.

Amazing!

We can call this an increase in humanity's potential. Everybody has worked with each other - both directly and indirectly - to improve the collective DoGL.

Progressing through collaboration is the best way to progress, because everybody is satisfied with the amount of DoGL that they and - maybe even more important - everyone else has.

No one has much more than anyone else, so there's no need to be jealous.

No one has much less, so no need to be protective.

As time goes by

Peight and Penine start to feel that they deserve more.

After all, they do supply the food. Everybody needs food. If they were to stop supplying food, everybody's DoGL would drop like a rock because they would have to go back to hunting on their own.

In a way, it was their partnership that enabled the rapid increase in DoGL enjoyed by all.

They started it.

Why shouldn't they have a better life?

After all, they do supply the food...

Peight and Penine make a new agreement. They agree that because they control the means of (food) production, they should have a higher DoGL than all those benefitting from their work.

Except Russel. He's such a funny guy, and they don't want to lose the entertainment.

They tell Pseven, who developed the infrustructure for food delivery to Peone through Pesix - we can call them the masses - that times are tough and that more resources are needed in order to keep everyone fed.

Pseven gives the news to the masses on his next delivery round. They become afraid that food may run low, and agree to help in any way they can. Besides, they have no reason to doubt Peight and Penine. They get together and devise a plan in accordance with Peight and Penine's demands.

Peone agrees to take on the duties of his friends - in addition to his own - as they work overtime to keep the food supply stable.

Pefour begins reading up on how to be more efficient in farming, hunting, taming animals, and construction. He serves as an advisor to Pefive, Petwo, and Pesix.

Pefive draws up plans for a magnificent facility that is capable of producing food up the ying yang.

Petwo gets it built on time and under budget.

Pesix helps to domesticate several new species of animal. He even learned from Pefour that crops can be crossbred to have more desirable traits, such as size.

Pethree, now a master of operations, increases the efficiency of all aspects of food production so that the task now requires less time, but provides much, much more.

After several years of toil, the new food production system is up and running. Peight and Penine thank everyone for their sacrifice, and that because of their hard work, there will likely be no food shortages in the coming years.

Peight and Penine, their jobs now almost completely automated, sit back and enjoy they fruits of their comrades' labor. And Russel's jokes, of course.

How great a DoGL they now have.

Pseven, who frequents the facility to pick up food, overhears Russel making a joke about the small mindedness of the masses. Penine and Peight erupt in laughter. Intrigued, he creeps closer to get a better listen.

As the laughter subsides, Pseven overhears:

Can you believe they fell for it? They gave up so much of their lives because they thought the food was running short. We didn't even have to feig a shortage. They willingly helped us reach this elite status! Hahahaha

Flabbergasted, Pseven storms in and confronts the triad. He threatens to tell everybody what they did.

The triad can't have that happen. If the masses found out about the deception, they would revolt.

They make another deal. The triad agrees to elevate Pseven's DoGL as long as he can keep a secret.

The truth always comes to light

If you have ever told a lie, you know that it never ends well for you. It might take a day, a month, a year, or ever several generations, but the truth will come out.

That is because people have an innate ability to detect when something is amiss. To know this, just look within. You certainly have come across many things in your life that just didn't feel right.

You may have this feeling today, that something is very, very wrong in the world, but you can't put your finger on it.

Either way, you know how this story ends. Pseven and above have become children of the lie told by the elite that control the means of production. The details don't matter much at this point, the story can only end one way.

Twenty years of progress wiped away in a single fit of collective rage, and Pefour got his eyes poked out.

The force that binds us together

The best way for humanity to progress is to work together.

Where we go one we go all.

Death is counterproductive. Fighting is counterproductive. Hatred is counterproductive. We get nowhere with it - no, we go backwards with it.

So why do we do it?

Take a look at the DoGL chart right before the revolt:

The "elite" are too far above the average DoGL, and the "masses" are too far below. (sup: Another very useful political term that serves to create a division where there is not one naturally) In such an environment, the average gets stuck. It cannot move much higher unless Peone through Pesix increase their DoGL. But as long as they are deceived into having a scarcity mindset, that will not happen.

Humanity is stuck.

There are many different ways DoGL can be measured. You could say that the more wealthy you are, the more DoGL you have. You could equate the number of options you have, or the amount of free time you have in your life to DoGL.

A higher DoGL is what we are all looking for. We all want to increase our DoGL.

The ones on the right are already working on raising the avg.
